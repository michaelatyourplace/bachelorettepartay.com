Planning Your Lowcountry Bachelorette Party

A guide to adult-friendly entertainment for groups 18 and over.

About This Guide

Finding the perfect bachelorette party activities can be a challenge, especially when some members of your group may be under 21. This guide is designed to help you plan a fun and memorable celebration in the Lowcountry that everyone can enjoy, focusing on ideas that are 18+ friendly.

From exciting nightlife to unique local experiences, the Lowcountry offers a wide range of options for your bride tribe. We've compiled a list of ideas that go beyond the typical bar scene, ensuring a fantastic time for all.

Adult-Friendly Bachelorette Ideas (18+)

Charleston Pub Crawl Nightlife

While many bars require patrons to be 21, plenty of venues and activities cater to the 18+ crowd. Consider a self-guided pub crawl that includes places with live music, entertainment, or a great atmosphere that doesn't strictly focus on alcohol consumption. Your group can enjoy the vibe and non-alcoholic drinks while still being part of the action.

Private Entertainment

For a truly private and memorable experience, consider booking private entertainment at your rental. This can be a great option for groups with members under 21, as it allows you to control the environment and ensure everyone is included. From male dancers to other types of performers, you can find a service that caters to your group's taste and comfort level.

Mobile Yoga and Wellness

A mobile yoga or wellness session is a fantastic way to bond with your bride tribe. These services come directly to your location, whether it's your Airbnb, a park, or a scenic beach. A morning yoga session can be a great way to start the day, and it's an activity that everyone, regardless of age, can participate in.

Drag Shows and Themed Events

Check out local venues that host 18+ or all-ages drag shows and themed events. These can be a fun and high-energy way to celebrate. Always verify the age requirements of the specific event or venue before booking.

Lowcountry Bachelorette Entertainers

Check out a male strippers in Charleston, Michael At Your Place that host 18+ or all-ages male dancer shows and themed events. These can be a fun and high-energy way to celebrate Vegas style. Always verify the age requirements of the specific event or private parties before booking.
